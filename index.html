About Us

S&N Prime Properties

At S&N Prime Properties, we believe your stay should be a journey of discovery, not just a place to rest. We create vibrant, thoughtfully designed accommodations that spark joy, inspire comfort, and leave a lasting impression.

Our spaces are more than just places to sleep — they're immersive escapes filled with personality, warmth, and a touch of the unexpected. Every retreat is carefully curated to deliver not just a stay, but an experience.

Founded by Sathwik Reddy — a UK property entrepreneur who built a portfolio from zero investment — S&N Prime Properties embodies the belief that premium accommodation should be personal and accessible to everyone.
